Chicken sandwich recipe

Menu

Description

This grilled chicken sandwich is simple to make with flavorsome marinated chicken, lettuce, tomato, and mayo. Crispy golden pan-fried bread makes it extra special! Add bacon and provolone cheese if you like.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 8 thick slices French bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, or to taste
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 sprigs fresh dill, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Prick chicken breasts all over with a fork, then place in a large bowl. Add 1/4 cup olive oil, white wine vinegar, lemon juice, dill, garlic and herb seasoning, and black pepper to the bowl.
  3. Remove chicken breasts from marinade; discard marinade.
  4. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, 10 to 15 minutes per side.
  5. Melt butter and 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Sprinkle garlic powder and salt over butter and oil.
  6. For sandwich assembly, place 1 French bread slice, toasted-side down, onto a serving plate. Spread mayonnaise on bread; top with a tomato slice, then season with salt and pepper. Cover with a grilled chicken breast half and lettuce leaf.
